Setup

Up and running in two minutes

Requires macOS with Apple Silicon (M1+).

  • 1 Download the .dmg and drag myOS to Applications.
  • 2 If macOS says the app is "damaged", open Terminal and run: $ xattr -cr /Applications/myOS.app Click to copy
  • 3 Open the app. Grant Full Disk Access when prompted (needed to read iMessage).
  • 4 Sign in to Gmail and connect an AI provider (Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini) during setup for the daily digest.
