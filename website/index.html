How to install

  • 1 Download the DMG for your Mac.
  • 2 Open the DMG and drag myOS to your Applications folder.
  • 3 Right-click the app and select Open (required for unsigned apps).
  • 4 Grant Full Disk Access when prompted to enable iMessage reading.
