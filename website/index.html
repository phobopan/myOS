Setup

Up and running in two minutes

Requires macOS with Apple Silicon (M1 or later).

  • 1 Download the DMG from GitHub Releases.
  • 2 Open the DMG and drag myOS to your Applications folder.
  • 3 Right-click the app and select Open — required on first launch for unsigned apps.
  • 4 Grant Full Disk Access when prompted to enable iMessage reading.
